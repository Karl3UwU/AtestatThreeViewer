Welcome to the GLTF Viewer project, made by Cotelea Carlo. To use this tool, you will need to export your 3D objects in a .gltf format. To load a .gltf file, drag & drop said file into the drop-zone. If you do not have any files to use, you can load the preview file by pressing on the "use preview" button. After loading, on pc, you can rotate with left-click, zoom with the scroll wheel and pan with right-click, and on mobile, you can rotate with one touch drag, and by using two touches you can pinch to zoom and pan by moving your fingers.
